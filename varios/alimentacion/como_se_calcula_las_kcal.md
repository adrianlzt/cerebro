# Calculo de kcal
http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/how-do-food-manufacturers/

Por cada gramo de tipo de elemento se cuentan ciertas kcal:
4 Kcal/g for protein
4 Kcal/g for carbohydrate
9 Kcal/g for fat
Alcohol is calculated at 7 Kcal/g.

Estos valores se calculan quemando estos elementos y midiendo la temperatura del agua que elevan (la energía que generan al quemarlos)

# Composición de los alimentos
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=11796381

Brief searching suggests that proteins are measured with a nitrogen content test and fats are determined by mixing the food with a non-polar solvent to see how much dissolves. Carbohydrates seem trickier and I haven't found a great explanation. Water solubility might work once the other components are removed.
